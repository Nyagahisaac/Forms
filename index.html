WELCOME TO THE ANKAN WEBSITE

The Ankana Website

This website application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender inputs their Akan Name will appear on click of the submit button.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
